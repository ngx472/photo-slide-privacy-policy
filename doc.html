Photo Slide

Most photos downloaded from internet are missing date and location information. This app lets you correct this. It allows to create Google-Photos albums that can be shared with your family and friends in which all photos have accurate date and location information

‚úÖ Multiple Selection

When you select several photos, changing the date or the location will affect all selected photos.

  • You enter the 'Selection Mode' by long press a photo or through the Select All menu
  • In 'Selection Mode':
    • tapping a photo will add it or remove it from the selection
    • a long press on photo will extend or remove the selection to this point (as Shift+click on Desktop)

ü™ú Adjust Date in Range

If you have a single contiguous selection of at least 3 photos, you can adjust the date for all selected photos.
It will leave the date of the first photo and the date of the last photo unchanged and adjust equally the dates on the photos in between.
This quickly lets you spread the dates of a range of photos.
