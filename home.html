photo_slide

Effective Date:May 30th 2025

Welcome to photo_slide.

This app lets you add date and location to your photos and save them to a Google Photo album

✅ Select photos (Menu)

✅ Long press to enter selection mode Press and hold on a photo to select it and enter selection mode

✅ Swipe to the right to remove photo

✅ Click the Date widget to change the date of a single photo or selected photos

✅ Use the side handle to reorder photos. The average date of the surrounding photos determines the new date

✅ Click the Location widget to change the date of a single photo or selected photos

✅ Save photos to a Google Photo album (Menu)
